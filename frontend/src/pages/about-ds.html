About DeepScan

DeepScan is a tool designed to help job seekers enhance their resumes by providing AI-powered text extraction and rewriting capabilities. Upload your resume, paste your text, or specify your field of work to get a polished, professional resume tailored to your needs.

Our mission is to make job applications easier and more effective. Stay tuned for more features as DeepScan continues to evolve!
